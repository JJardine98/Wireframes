Process Store Order

As soon as an order is being proccessed, the View Store order status for that order will change to processing.

Order Information

When a Barcode is scanned by the user, the SKU, Item Name and Unit Price is automatically added to the list, and the quantity is updated by 1.
Subsequent scanning of the same Barcode will increment the quantity further.
